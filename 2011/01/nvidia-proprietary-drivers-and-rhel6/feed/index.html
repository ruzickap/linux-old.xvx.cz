]]> By: Nathan http://linux.xvx.cz/2011/01/nvidia-proprietary-drivers-and-rhel6/comment-page-1/#comment-291 Nathan Thu, 06 Oct 2011 15:44:11 +0000 http://linux.xvx.cz/?p=981#comment-291 Thank you so much Petr! I had followed another guide for my initial installation of the NVIDIA drivers on RHEL6 and all was well until a kernel update, at which point X wouldn’t start. I followed your guide and it worked like a charm. One thing I’ll say for anyone else having this issue: since I had removed the Nouveau drivers, after the kernel update X couldn’t start. The NVIDIA installer modifies the kernel, so I had to load the new kernel with init 3 enabled, and reinstall the NVIDIA drivers. This reinstall was critical for me, nothing else seemed to work and X would fail to start. Now I am sitting here typing in a browser with the new kernel enabled.
